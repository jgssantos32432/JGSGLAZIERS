JGS Glaziers was founded in July 14, 2007 by it's current head Jimmy Gaspar Santos, in his eyes this business was not about the money or success but about his maturity and growth as a person and as a father wishing to train his children and inherent his work. This is a family owned business and every staff is blood related to one another. We prioritize perfection and durability of each craft, ensuring a greater service everytime, we chose the security and comfort of our customers since they are the reason for the growth of this.

The Founder is incharge of managing the flow of work in the business, managing the needed supply and the leader of the operations and the verifiers of new ideas.

Glazier

The Glazier is incharge of making the products, measuring, and the one's engeneering and creating the products you buy, they take orders from the Founder and Secretary.

Secretary

The Secretary is incharge of managing the supply, this website and marketing, they only take charge from the Founder's, and are the second in command.

Technology Support

The Technology Support is incharge of the machines, online sources, ideas and execution of ideas, ther also made this website and help in marketing.
