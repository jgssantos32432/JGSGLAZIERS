Aquarium

Aquarium are made purely from glass with an aluminum frame. Our aquariums are only limited to a rectangular or cube shape. An aquarium uses water, with this in mind we have to secure the edges with aluminum and silicon and use thick glass. We do not provide any aquarium products, but we do take requests like the aquarium size or a cover for the aquarium.

Terrarium

Terrarium are made purely from glass with an aluminum frame. Our terrariums are only limited to a rectangular or cube shape. A terrarium sometimes have animals or insects and they have plants, with this in mind we have to secure the edges with aluminum and silicon and we can use thin glass. We do not provide any terrarium products, but we do take requests like the terrarium size or a cover for the terrarium.
